Paragraphs

Lesson 2 of 4
Use <p> tags for readable text blocks. Browsers automatically add space before and after paragraphs. Perfect for articles, descriptions, and content. 
<p>This is a paragraph.</p>
<p>Another paragraph with more text.</p>

Line Breaks

Lesson 3 of 4
<br> creates line breaks without paragraph spacing. Perfect for addresses, poems, song lyrics, or anywhere you need tight line control. 
<p>Line 1<br>
Line 2<br>
Line 3</p>
